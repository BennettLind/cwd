This Website is about the movie Cars

The movie is about a famous, rookie sensation race car named Lightning Mcqueen. The movie starts with a three-way tie, whichs makes them have to travel across the country for their final race to determine the winner. On the way to the next race Mcqueen gets lost from his personal truck and finds himself in trouble with a local town called Radiator Springs. Here he meets his soon to be best friend and makes up to the locals after destroying their town. He meets a old famous race car that teaches him drifitng and how to drive smarter. From there he goes to The Piston Cup where he beats the other two cars and wins. The Movie ends with Mcqueen going back to radiator springs after he promised mator he would come back and not forget the connections he made that helped him win.
